Travel & Stay

Getting to Savannah

The nearest airport is Savannah/Hilton Head International (SAV). Rideshare, taxis, and rental cars are all easily available.

If you’re driving, there are multiple parking options near downtown. We recommend giving yourself a little extra time to enjoy the charm of the city.

Where to Stay

We recommend staying in or near the Historic District for easy access to the venues and all the cobblestone, squares, and Spanish moss.

  • Historic District
  • Near River Street
  • Forsyth Park area

We’ll share any hotel block information and discount codes here as soon as they’re confirmed.
